Kyowa Kirin Hong Kong

Kyowa Kirin Hong Kong strives to contribute to the health and well-being of people in Hong Kong and Macau by creating new value through the pursuit of advances in life sciences and technologies.


Kyowa Kirin Hong Kong was established in 1993. Since our beginnings to the year 2002, we have focused on sales and marketing functions in Mainland China. From 2003, we commenced sales activities in Hong Kong and Macau.

As part of the Kyowa Kirin Group headquartered in Japan, we are dedicated to pursuing our Commitment to Life as an integral part of our work. Over the years, Kyowa Kirin has invested in our efforts to use innovation and technological advances to further enhance the patient experience and patient value in the lesser-known therapeutic areas.

Message from the General Manager

Welcome to our new Kyowa Kirin Hong Kong website.


Through this website, we will provide you information about our philosophy, core values, and most importantly, our focus on science.


Kyowa Kirin Hong Kong Co., Limited is one of the subsidiary companies of Kyowa Kirin Asia Pacific Pte Ltd. under the Kirin Holdings Company Limited in Japan. Since our establishment in 1993, our therapeutic focus areas have been on Nephrology, Oncology, Immunology & Allergy, and Central Nervous System.


Kyowa Kirin Hong Kong is striving to contribute to the health and well-being of people in Hong Kong and Macau by creating new value through the pursuit of advancement in life sciences and technologies. Our vision is to become an indispensable specialized pharmaceutical company by bringing patients hope and smile to life.


If you have any enquiries, please feel free to contact us. Your suggestions, ideas, and thoughts are important to us.


We look forward to hearing from you soon.

Best Regards

Alvin Yum

General Manager, Kyowa Kirin Hong Kong

Our Vision

As a Japan-based Global Specialty Pharmaceutical Company, Kyowa Kirin Hong Kong is dedicated towards our patient health and wellbeing worldwide. We leverage on innovative drug discovery and global commercialisation, driven by state-of-the-art antibody technologies mainly in the core therapeutic areas of Oncology, Nephrology, Immunology & Allergy, and Central Nervous System.
